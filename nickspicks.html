Nick's Picks - Books

Welcome to Nick's Picks, book edition! Today we'll be going over my top three favorite books:

  1. The Chronicles of Narnia
  2. A Wrinkle in Time
  3. Something Wicked This Way Comes

The Chronicles of Narnia

This classic series kicked off the children's fantasy genre and still holds up today. I love it for the complex and poignant themes it tackles. Each book feels like a life lesson wrapped in magic.

A Wrinkle in Time

Another classic children's fantasy book, this time with a sci-fi bent! A Wrinkle in Time delivers a compelling story about love, loss, and the nature of the cosmos in a tale of angels, stars, darkness, and power.

Something Wicked This Way Comes

And now for something completally different. Something Wicked This Way Comes is by no means a children's book, and its magic is of a far more sinister bent. It's a tale about a menacing carnival that comes to town and two boys that get caught up in its complexities.
